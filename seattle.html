Life in Seattle

My neighborhood

This summer, I was doing an internship as a production engineer at Meta Platforms, Inc. The company offered me a coorporate housing during the summer and it was on Pike Street. It was very different from Ann Arbor, where I am pursuing my Master's Degree. This area is very modernized and it has a whole bunch of restaurants and tall buildings. One can also find great theatres, shopping malls and enjoyable cinemas in this place.

One thing I noticed in Seatlle is that many local people love to bring their kayaks or paddle boards to the lake during nice days. Even there are many motorized boats driving on the lake, they can still find a spot to fit in and enjoy their weekend.
