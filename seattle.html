Life in Seattle

My neighborhood

This summer, I was doing an internship as a production engineer at Meta Platforms, Inc. The company offered me a coorporate housing during the summer and it was on Pike Street. It was very different from Ann Arbor, where I am pursuing my Master's Degree. This area is very modernized and it has a whole bunch of restaurants and tall buildings. One can also find great theatres, shopping malls and enjoyable cinemas in this place.

Lake Union in Seattle

One thing I noticed in Seatlle is that many local people love to bring their kayaks or paddle boards to the lake during nice days. Even there are many motorized boats driving on the lake, they can still find a spot to fit in and enjoy their weekend. During one weekend, I was hanging out with my friends on a rental yacht, and the captain told us that Lakes in Seattle are always so crowded due to high volume of all kinds of boats. Thus, it was not safe to just jump into the water and start swimming in some areas.

Q: How long does it take to travel from Seattle downtown to the Olympic national park?


A: There are two main routes to get there. One is to take travel purely on the ground, while the other is to take a ferry to Bainbridge Island and then drive to the park. Interestingly, both of them takes approximately the same time, 2.5 hours. The ferry is a little bit more expensive, but it is a more scenic route, especially during the night.
